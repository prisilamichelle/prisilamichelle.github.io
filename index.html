Reality Technology and Tourism Industry

By Prisila Michelle - 13516129

Technology is changing the travel and tourism industry. Reality technology such as augmented, virtual, and mixed reality offer several new ways to improve tourism industry and travel experience.

What is augmented, virtual, and mixed reality?

Augmented Reality

A technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view. Currently, augmented reality is the most mainstream reality technology, one of the reason being the release of Pok√©mon Go in 2016.

Virtual Reality

The computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional image or environment that can be interacted with in a seemingly real or physical way by a person using special electronic equipment, such as a helmet with a screen inside or gloves fitted with sensors. Nowadays, there are lots of virtual reality viewer, such as Google Cardboard, HTC Vive Pro, Oculus Rift, and many more.

Mixed Reality

The merging of real and virtual worlds to produce new environments and visualizations where physical and digital objects co-exist and interact in real time. One of the most popular mixed reality viewer is HoloLens by Microsoft. HoloLens is the first self-contained, holographic computer, that enables us to engage with our digital content and interact with holograms in the world around us.

How would they improve the tourism industry?

Hotel

Hotels can advertise themselves using reality technology. This also help customers to actually see how a hotel is really like, so they can choose the best hotel according to their interest.

Museum

Museums can provide interactive learning apps that enhance visitors experience. Persons with mobility impairment could see the entire museum without having to move much.

Travel Agency

Travel agency can provide the preview of locations in their travel package. This would prevent disappointment and maybe would attract more customer to buy their travel package.

Navigation

In foreign country, it could be hard to ask for directions if we don't understand the language. Cities can provide navigation to hotels and popular tourist places to help tourists navigate to the place they want to visit.

Tourist Attraction

Tourist attraction can be made more interesting with mixed reality viewer or augmented reality apps. Tourist can rent mixed reality viewer as their personal and interactive tour guide, or they could download the tourist attraction app.
